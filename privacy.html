privacy policy

how we handle your data with care

last updated: june 16, 2025

introduction

dammie is your guide to discovering amsterdam's best events and experiences. this privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

information we collect

personal information you provide

  • profile information: name, age, amsterdam postcode
  • contact information: dutch phone number (for account verification)
  • preferences: your interests ("pulse"), favorite amsterdam places, and personal insights
  • profile picture: optional photo you choose to upload

information we collect automatically

  • location data: your approximate location in amsterdam to calculate cycling distances to event venues
  • app usage: which events you view, save, or share
  • device information: basic device and app performance data

calendar access

  • when you save events, we may add them to your device's calendar (with your permission)
  • we only write calendar events - we don't read your existing calendar data

how we use your information

we use your information to:

  • personalize event recommendations based on your interests and location
  • show cycling distances from your location to event venues
  • save your favorite events and maintain your profile
  • verify your amsterdam phone number during account setup
  • add saved events to your calendar (when requested)

information sharing

we do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. your data stays private and is only used to enhance your dammie experience.

the only exception is anonymous, aggregated usage data that cannot identify you individually.

data storage and security

  • your profile data is stored securely on your device and our servers
  • we use industry-standard security practices to protect your information
  • your location data is only used for distance calculations and is not permanently stored
  • you can delete your account and data at any time

your rights and choices

you can:

  • edit your profile information at any time through the app
  • control location access through your device settings
  • manage calendar permissions through your device settings
  • delete your account by contacting us

children's privacy

dammie is intended for users 18 and older. we do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

changes to this policy

we may update this privacy policy occasionally. we'll notify you of any significant changes through the app or by email.

questions about privacy?

if you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact us at:
